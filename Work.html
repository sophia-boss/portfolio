First, what is Triton XR?
Triton XR is an award-winning professional and social organization at UC San Diego, one of the largest and most successful XR student organizations in the nation, and the best resource for undergraduate students to engage with XR technology in San Diego. The organization holds numerous events including Hackathons, large-scale XR demos, workshops, project showcases, socials, and collaborations with other student organizations and departments on campus.
The Animation Studio is a branch of Triton XR, and has been at UCSD for multiple years.
