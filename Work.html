What is Triton XR?

Triton XR is an award-winning professional and social organization at UC San Diego, one of the largest and most successful XR student organizations in the nation, and the best resource for undergraduate students to engage with XR technology in San Diego.
The Animation Studio is a branch of Triton XR, and has been at UCSD for multiple years.

Bodylogical

Augmented reality utilizes interactivity with the environment to produce a new level of immersion in a subject matter. Bodylogical utilizes this immersion to educate users about the negative and positive heath effects of various physical habits. It aims to interact with the real world, to show that various topics of heath management are in fact real and affect our every day lives.

What I did...
My role was to develop 3D assets, create rigs, animate the models, and design the over all visual elements of the app.
