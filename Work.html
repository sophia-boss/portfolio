Bodylogical

Bodylogical is an interactive AR data visualization application for the iphone ans ipad. It allows users to predict their future health levels based off of components like blood pressure, alcohol consumption, sleep, and physical exercise. The results are visualized with animated models.

Sticky Pals

Sticky Pals is a desktop web app that allows you to create “walls” of sticky notes. The idea is that it enables people to have a more modular way of taking notes. It also has a feature where you are able to publish your walls and explore a community of users to view other people’s walls/notes.
