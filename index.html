My name is Sophia Boss, I am an undergrad at UC San Diego majoring in Interdisciplinary Computing in the Arts and minoring in user interaction design and computer science.

It is taken for granted that technology has intersected aspects of nearly every discipline, and is constantly involved with our everyday lives. I create accessibile art by use of digital media, so an experience can be shared from one person to another with a touch of a fingertip.
